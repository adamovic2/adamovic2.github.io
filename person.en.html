Situating Identities of LGBTIQ migrants from Serbia and Bosnia in Austria

Introducing myself

My name is Slađana Adamović. I was born in Carinthia (Austria) and I am currently 24 years old. My parents moved seperately to Austria and met each other in Carinthia.

Together we often visited my grandparents in Serbia, because after the war they moved from Kosovo and Bosnia to different parts in Serbia.

I am living in Vienna since 2014 and fell in love with the city. I met a lot of people here and was able to connect in some LGBT communities and to connect with some there too.

Because of my friends and my diaspora life, I wanted to look deeper in the experiences and stories of LGBTIQ migrants from Serbia and Bosnia in Austria, which for me always seemed to be fallen out of a category.
