Situating Identities of LGBTIQ migrants from Serbia and Bosnia in Austria

Introducing myself

My name is Slađana Adamović. I was born in Carinthia (Austria) and I am currently 24 years old. My parents moved seperately to Austria and met each other in Carinthia.

Together we often visited my grandparents in Serbia, because after the Yugoslav war they moved from Kosovo and Bosnia to different parts in Serbia.

I moved to Vienna 2014 to study cultural and social anthropology at the university of Vienna and I fell in love with the city. Here was able to join LGBTIQ events and visited LGBTIQ organization and connected with some there too.

Because of my friends and my diaspora life, I wanted to look deeper onto the experiences and stories of LGBTIQ migrants from Serbia and Bosnia in Austria, because it seemed that they always fell out of simple categories.
